
# Interests — Energy-Based Interest Manager

A simple, clean website that helps you manage your interests based on **energy**, not importance.

Inspired by the idea that the best time to do something hard isn't when it's due — it's when you have the energy for it.

---

## The Idea

Most productivity systems tell you to prioritise by urgency or importance. This one asks a different question: **how much energy does this actually take?**

1. **Categorise your interests by energy** — High 🔥, Medium ⚡, or Low 🌿
2. **Focus on two per week** — depth over breadth, rotated weekly
3. **Match them to your day** — set your energy each morning and see what fits

---

## Features

- Add and manage personal interests with an energy level tag
- Pick 2 weekly focus interests — auto-resets each new week
- Set your daily energy and get matched suggestions from your weekly focus
- All data saved locally in the browser — no account, no server, no cost

---

## Tech Stack

- HTML
- CSS
- Vanilla JavaScript
- `localStorage` for persistent data across sessions

No frameworks. No dependencies. One file.
